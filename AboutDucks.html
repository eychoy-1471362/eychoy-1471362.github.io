About Rubber Ducks

Everything you need to know about our rubber duckies!

RubberDucks.com was established in 2020. Our vision was to create a company that would allow people to buy rubber ducks. Everyone should deserve to have a rubber duck with them during bathtime.

The founders of the company have had a passion for rubber ducks ever since they were young. They wanted a world where everyone could play with a rubber duck during bathtime.

As said by many visionaries in the time before us, "No one should be denied access to rubber duckies, the most basic universal human right there is!"
